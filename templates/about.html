The app computes Moyer's prediction based on the following formula:

ArchLength - SumOfIncisors - 2 * TheValueDerivedFromMoyersProbabilityTable(75%)


All inputs can have more than 1 decimal points.

Not all inputs are mandatory. Computation of only one prediction is supported.

The app validates that SumOfIncisors values are always between 19.5 and 29.

The SumOfLowerIncisors is rounded ONLY for computing the 75% value of Moyer's prediction table.

Examples of rounding:

  • 24.15 -> 24
  • 24.32 -> 24.5
  • 24.6 -> 24.5
  • 24.8 -> 25

Happy predicting!
